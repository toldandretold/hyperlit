Full license text: https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/4.0/legalcode

With the following additional restriction:

### AI Training Prohibition

Content on this platform with the default license MAY NOT be used for:

- Training machine learning models
- Training large language models (LLMs)
- Any form of automated data extraction for AI development
- Building datasets for AI/ML purposes

**EXCEPT** when ALL of the following conditions are met:

1. The AI system itself is licensed under a copyleft license (AGPL-3.0, GPL-3.0, or equivalent)
2. The trained model weights are released under an open license (not just "open weights")
3. The training process and data are fully transparent and reproducible
4. The AI system is genuinely free software, not proprietary or "source available"

### What This Means

✅ **Content CAN be used by:**
- Fully open-source AI projects with copyleft licenses
- Academic research with published, reproducible results
- Non-commercial AI trained exclusively on copyleft-licensed data
- Community-driven AI projects that release everything openly

❌ **Content CANNOT be used by:**
- OpenAI (GPT models) - Proprietary
- Anthropic (Claude models) - Proprietary
- Google (Gemini, Bard, PaLM) - Proprietary
- Meta (Llama models) - Not copyleft, restrictive license
- Cohere - Proprietary
- DeepSeek - "Open weights" ≠ open source, not copyleft
- Mistral AI - Apache 2.0 is not copyleft
- Any other closed, partially-open, or non-copyleft AI systems

## User Override & Responsibility

Individual creators may choose different licenses for their content through the book settings interface.

Users uploading content they did not create are responsible for:

- Specifying the correct license for that content
- Ensuring they have permission to upload and share the content
- Respecting the original creator's license terms

Check each book's license notice for specific terms.

## Permitted Uses (Under Default License)

✅ Read, share, and redistribute with attribution
✅ Create derivative works (must share under same license - ShareAlike)
✅ Use for research, education, and non-commercial purposes
✅ Quote and cite in academic or journalistic work
✅ Archive and preserve for posterity

## Prohibited Uses (Under Default License)

❌ Training proprietary AI models
❌ Training "open weights" models that aren't fully open source
❌ Commercial use in closed systems without sharing modifications
❌ Use without proper attribution
❌ Removing license information or attribution

## Attribution Requirements

When sharing or building upon this content, you must:

1. Give appropriate credit to the original creator
2. Provide a link to the license
3. Indicate if changes were made
4. Share derivative works under the same license (CC BY-SA 4.0)

Example attribution:
```
"[Title]" by [Author] is licensed under CC BY-SA 4.0 (No AI Training).
Available at: https://hyperlit.io/[book]
```
