Book to be published by **Feminist Education Association**

Proposed title: 

# Voices From the Frontlines: The Fight Against Fascism and the Far-Right 

Collection to have an introduction by Debbie which: 

Introduces the collection and its sections, including:
- explaining the difference between and the need to resist both right wing populism and outright fascists
- Discusses the vital contribution that the Freedom Socialist and Freedom Socialist Organiser play in synthesising the lessons from the struggle
- Underscores a Trotskyist analysis of fascism and the need for a united front 
- Includes an explanation of our CARF experience and our vision of what a united front needs to look like today (ie: broad/inclusive/not just comprised of the left, the importance of unions, essential its democratic)
- Highlights the depth of the experience resisting the right wing in its various guises that both FSP and RW have in Australia and internationally
- Invites readers to get in touch/involved/join

Collection to include:
