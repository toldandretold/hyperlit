# Commons

Property regimes vary widely [Ostrom 1990]. The tragedy framing [Hardin 1968] was later critiqued [Benkler 2006]. Some authors are cited together [Ostrom 1990; Benkler 2006].

## References

Ostrom, Elinor (1990). Governing the Commons. Cambridge University Press.

Hardin, Garrett (1968). The Tragedy of the Commons. Science, 162(3859).

Benkler, Yochai (2006). The Wealth of Networks. Yale University Press.
