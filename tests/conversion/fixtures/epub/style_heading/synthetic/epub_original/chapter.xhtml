CHAPTER ONE
p.1
The cathedral model is built slowly over centuries by many anonymous hands, each adding a small part to a whole that none of them will live to see completed in full.
The First Section
The bazaar model by contrast is noisy and chaotic, a marketplace of competing voices where order emerges from the bottom up rather than being imposed from above by a single architect.
The Second Section
Both models have their place, and the wise builder learns when each is appropriate to the task at hand and the materials and people available.
CHAPTER TWO
A new chapter begins, and with it a new set of concerns that build upon what came before while opening fresh questions of their own for the reader to consider.
